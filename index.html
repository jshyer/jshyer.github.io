Lumin is a game about bringing fallen stars back into the sky. Play as Lumin, the bearer of light, and collect fallen stars that now inhabit a deserted excavation site. Explore a world powered by the light of the stars and uncover a rich past of a civilization now gone. Can you bring the light home?
